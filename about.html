Why Choose Us?

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to provide comprehensive orthopaedic care. From a 24×7 trauma and emergency unit to advanced digital X-ray and diagnostic imaging, minor OT and day-care surgical suites, and a 10-bedded inpatient orthopaedic unit, every facility is designed to ensure safe treatment, accurate diagnosis, and smooth recovery. Additionally, our personalised physiotherapy and rehabilitation centre helps patients regain mobility quickly and confidently.

We treat a wide range of conditions, including fractures, joint problems, spine disorders, sports injuries, arthritis, and post-traumatic complications.
